Capstone Project

Finwell Apps


Finwell Apps is a budgeting software developed for MoneyTree Software that will be utilized by the organization Habitat for Humanity in classrooms to teach principles of financial wellness to residents of Delaware County. Finwell Apps seeks to teach financial literacy at an elementary level by providing simplistic and straightforward functionality with a streamlined UI. Sophisticated financial resources are provided throughout the userâ€™s experience with the app to further enforce financial responsibility and growth.

Tech Stack

Finwell Apps is developed using Xamarin.forms to be deployed natively on both iOS and Android platforms. It also utilizes SQLite to save user's data locally.

Features

Users of Finwell Apps will be able to create a budget and keep track of their expenses over a thirty day budgeting cycle. They will also be able to add savings goals to make progress towards at the end of their budgeting cycle. Resources will also be provided through the app to teach users mores specifically about financial wellness.

I also worked as the sort of Business Analyst between my team and the team at MoneyTree; I drafted documents and emails for the team at MoneyTree and took the lead in meetings and presentations with them.
